# Vital Synergy Brand Guidelines

Reference for all Vital Synergy work. Apply consistently across web, documents, and collateral.

---

## Colours

All text is black. Colour is reserved for UI, charts, and accents only.

| Name | Hex | RGB | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Deep Blue | #124B8A | 18, 75, 138 | UI elements, buttons, visual highlights, charts (1st series). Never text. |
| Sky Blue | #2196D4 | 33, 150, 212 | Charts (2nd series), non-text callouts, UI highlights. Never text. |
| Indigo | #2F3382 | 47, 51, 130 | Charts (3rd series), deep accents, supporting visuals. Never text. |
| Black | #000000 | 0, 0, 0 | All text: body, headers, titles, links, callouts, labels, UI text. |
| Vital Orange | #EB5324 | 235, 83, 36 | Accent only. Small highlights, dividers, single emphasis. Never text or large areas. |

---

## Typography

| Role | Font | Case | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display | Heavitas | UPPERCASE | Posters, hero titles, large display moments |
| Subhead | Nexa Bold | Sentence or upper | Section headers, subtitles, calls to action |
| Body | Nexa Regular | Normal | Default body copy across proposals, web, collateral |
| Caption | Nexa Light | Normal | Captions, metadata, fine print |

---

## Logo Usage

| Surface | Treatment |
|---|---|
| White | Default. Body backgrounds, documents, light surfaces. |
| Deep Blue | Primary brand surface. Reversed logo. |
| Sky Blue | Secondary brand surface. Reversed logo. |

Do not stretch, skew, or recolour the logo. Do not place over busy imagery without contrast. Do not use orange as a primary headline or large body fill.

## Voice and Tone

| Principle | Description |
|---|---|
| Professional | Clear, concise, informed. No unnecessary jargon. |
| Approachable | Friendly and welcoming while retaining authority. |
| Reliable | Dependable, consistent, solution focused. |
| Forward-thinking | Innovative; current on industry advancements. |
| Community minded | Supportive of local and regional initiatives. |

### Tone by Scenario

| Scenario | Tone |
|---|---|
| Client proposals and tenders | Confident, precise, evidence-led. |
| Marketing collateral | Warm, benefits-led, plain English. |
| Social media | Conversational but never flippant. |
| Internal comms | Clear, supportive, action-oriented. |
| Crisis and sensitive | Calm, factual, empathetic. |

---

## Language Rules

- Spelling: UK English (organise, colour, programme)
- Punctuation: no em dashes; use commas, semicolons, full stops
- Voice: active ("Our team delivered..." not passive constructions)
- Acronyms: spell out on first use (M&E = Mechanical and Electrical)
- Numbers: spell out one to nine; figures for 10 and above
- Preferred terms: bespoke not tailored; expertise not experience
