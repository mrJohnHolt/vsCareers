Our Journey

The Vital Synergy Story

Founded in Lancashire, Vital Synergy has grown from a specialist M&E engineering company into one of the region's leading building services employers. The business was built on a commitment to quality, compliance and developing local engineering talent.

Vital Synergy operates across the full spectrum of Mechanical & Electrical engineering, from gas and HVAC to design, project management, procurement and compliance, placing skilled professionals into roles that match their expertise and ambition.

This recruitment portal represents the next step: a direct, transparent route for candidates to find their place within Vital Synergy and for operational teams to manage and grow their workforce efficiently.
